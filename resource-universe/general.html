    • WNCCHS is a private, non-profit, tax-exempt corporation based in Asheville, Buncombe County, North Carolina
    that provides comprehensive, high-quality whole person health care to our patients regardless of ability to pay. LGBTQ+ services are also offered here!
  • UNC Asheville Student Health Portal
    • Student portal which will allow you to interact with the Health and Counseling

  • Safe(r) Sex Supplies '21 - '22
    • Form to fill out that provides free birth control and various safe
    sex supplies for students at UNCA (on or off campus residency.)
  • Manna Food Helpline
    • If you are struggling with buying and having access to food, this helpline can provide
    you with referrals to food pantries, markets, meals and other resources.
  • Loving Food Resources
    • Loving Food Resources (LFR) is a food pantry that provides food, health, and personal care items to people
    in 18 counties of WNC who are living with HIV/AIDS or who are in Home Hospice care regardless of diagnosis.
