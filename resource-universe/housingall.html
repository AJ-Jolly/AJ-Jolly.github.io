All Housing Resources

  • UNCA Housing
    • UNCA Housing provides general information about dorms and the benefits of living on campus.
  • Governers Hall
    • Residence hall that features single and double rooms in suites and overlooks the soccer field.
  • Founders Hall
    • A seven-story residence hall, located near the Dining Hall and Highsmith Student Union, and features suite-style rooms.
  • The Village
    • Halls that offer single rooms for 100 students. Four rooms per suite that share bathroom and common areas.
  • Mills Hall
    • Suites with two bedrooms, a bathroom, and a den. Outside it features an outdoor volleyball court with grills.
  • Ponder Hall
    • 4-6 students can live in a suite that has singles and doubles with a common living area and large bathroom.
  • The Ridges
    • South and West Ridge are in a wooded area near the Botanical Gardens and
    house double rooms with connecting bathroom to another double room.
  • The Woods
    • Apartment style housing for upperclass students with laundry rooms on site and full kitchens in each apartment.
