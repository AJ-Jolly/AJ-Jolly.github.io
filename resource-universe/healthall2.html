All Health Resources

  • Range Urgent Care
    • Range Urgent care provides a range of servces (in person or virtual) for ailments
    such as illness and injury, among others. They take student health insurance.
  • Western North Carolina Community Health Services
    • WNCCHS is a private, non-profit, tax-exempt corporation based in Asheville, Buncombe County, North Carolina
    that provides comprehensive, high-quality whole person health care to our patients regardless of ability to pay. LGBTQ+ services are also offered here!
  • Community Family Pratice
    • Specializes in family medicine and nursing; experience with transgender patients.
  • Live Well in WNC
    • Holistic and preventative care, nutrition, supplements, fitness, and hormone replacement therapy.
  • The Pisgah Institute
    • The Pisgah Institute is comprised of psychiatrists, doctoral-level psychologists, psychiatric nurse practitioners, certified
    physician’s assistants, and master’s level clinical mental health counselors who address a full range of mental conditions.
  • Vaya Health
    • Vaya Health is a public managed care organization (MCO) that oversees Medicaid, federal, state and local funding
    for services and supports related to mental health, substance use disorder and intellectual/developmental disabilities.
  • UNC Asheville Student Health Portal
    • Student portal which will allow you to interact with the Health and Counseling
    staff and complete various functions related to your health care.
  • Safe(r) Sex Supplies '21 - '22
    • Form to fill out that provides free birth control and various safe
    sex supplies for students at UNCA (on or off campus residency.)
