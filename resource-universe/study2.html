Virtual Study Resources

  • Gimp
    • A free alternative to Photoshop
  • DaVinci Resolve
    • A free alternative to Premiere Pro with an arguably superior color correction system. Commonly used for major Hollywood productions
  • W3Schools
    • A teaching tool for a wide variety of programming languages including HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Python
  • Library Genesis
    • A file sharing site that lets you download textbooks, general interest books, scholarly articles, audiobooks, magazines, and comics for free
  • Noba Project
    • Psychology topics compiled by university professors in easyto read modules. Completely free to use
  • Sci-Hub
    • Provides free access to millions of research papers and books
  • Internet Archive
    • A massive free library of books, software, movies, archived webpages, music, and more
  • MyStudyLife
    • Helps keep track of assignments, exams, and class schedules.
    Allows you to sort assignments by level of priority and visualize how close they are to completion
